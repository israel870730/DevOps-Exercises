

Pasos:

Instalar ExternaDNS
kubectl apply -f Deploy-ExternaDNS/externaldns.yaml
kubectl get po -A | grep dns

Eliminar externalDNS:
kubectl delete -f Deploy-ExternaDNS/externaldns.yaml

Crear los ingress:
kubectl apply -f k8s/ingress/ingres-1.yaml
kubectl apply -f k8s/ingress/ingres-2.yaml

Revisar los ingress:
kubectl get ing -A

Eliminar ingress:
kubectl delete -f k8s/ingress/ingres-1.yaml 
kubectl delete -f k8s/ingress/ingres-2.yaml

Ver los log de externaldns:
kubectl logs -f deploy/external-dns -n kube-system

Reiniciar pod:
kubectl rollout restart deploy/external-dns -n kube-system
